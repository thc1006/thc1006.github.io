Technical Deep Dive (v1.0.4)

A closer look at the final model architecture, DP implementation, and hyperparameters.

Model & DP Architecture

The model is a 4-layer fully connected network that regresses `allocation_efficiency`. To implement Differential Privacy robustly, we don't rely on a DP-specific optimizer. Instead, a custom function wraps the standard client optimizer to perform two key steps before aggregation:

  • Gradient Clipping: Limits the influence of any single data point by capping the L2 norm of the gradients.
  • Noise Addition: Adds carefully calibrated Gaussian noise to the clipped gradients, providing a mathematical guarantee of privacy.
